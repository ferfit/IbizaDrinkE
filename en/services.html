SERVICES

We create the event you are imagining

Nuestra gran experiencia en el sector nos permite planificar todo tipo de eventos

Our staff at your disposal

We have excellent professionals, including event managers, bartenders, waiters and bar assistants fully trained to ensure everything goes perfectly. Our priority is to satisfy our client’s needs and outstand for our team-working abilities. We are up to date with the latest trends without overlooking classic mixology, certainly the basis of our profession.

Mobile
Bars

We are fans of details so we offer our exclusive mobile bars that can be moved around Ibiza. It’s a project that was developed to fulfill the needs of our team and provide our exclusive service wherever you need it. Our bars are handmade by a renowned carpenter of the island. The final result becomes highly functional bars with minimalist details, fully prepared to cover small and large scale events.

Exclusive
locations

If you don’t have a place to host your celebration, we can assist you with a wide range of real state professionals to help you find a land or sea location that suits your needs.

Barras Móviles

Somos fanáticos de los detalles, de modo que, ponemos a tu disposición exclusivas barras trasportables por toda Ibiza. Es un proyecto que nace para cubrir las necesidades de nuestro equipo, y así poder brindar un servicio exclusivo en donde tú lo desees. Nuestras barras son confeccionadas, completamente a mano, por un reconocido carpintero de la isla. El resultado final son barras funcionales con acabados minimalistas, habilitadas para cubrir pequeños y grandes eventos.
